About

SALARY STRUGGLE?

Salary Struggle is a game made for everyone who enjoys a fun little challenge. The game is largely luck based, but there are definitely strategies to help you get ahead. Explore and expand your knowledge and spend your energy and money wisely. A top 10 result will get you on the leaderboard.

GAME SETTING

The game takes place in a fictional city, lightly inspired by Tokyo, where you play as an unfortunate soul who has piles of debt. The only way forward is to get a job as a self-taught programmer within 30 days. This is no easy task, and you need more than coding skills to make it.

GAMEPLAY

You have 30 days to get a job as a programmer. To succeed you need to pass four interviews. Each day is limited by your energy. You can do activities or perform tasks that increases your stats, but everything requires energy, and sometimes money. Head home to sleep to replenish your energy, but be aware that certain activities give you penalties, e.g. drinking will give you an energy hangover.
